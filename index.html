Five systems powering every session — for food microbiology, law, finance, or anything else.

01 — Adaptive Bloom Engine
Tracks mastery at three levels independently per topic: recall, application, synthesis. Targets your weakest Bloom level, not just your weakest topic.
02 — Error Taxonomy Classifier
Every wrong answer is classified: concept gap, exam trap, recall failure, or misapplication. Patterns surface in analytics so you fix the right thing.
03 — Deep Drill Protocol
Concept primer → 3 recall → 3 application → 3 synthesis. Topic isn't mastered until you clear all three Bloom levels above 75%.
04 — Dual Mode UI
Exam Mode: clinical black, countdown timer, no explanations until you answer. Mastery Mode: green-tinted, concept primers, Bloom badges, insight after every question.
05 — Diagnostic Study Planner
Baseline assessment seeds your mastery map. Plan adapts daily based on weak topics, hours available, and days to exam.
